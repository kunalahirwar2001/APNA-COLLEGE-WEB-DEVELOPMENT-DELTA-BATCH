Iron Man

Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was created by writer and editor Stan Lee, developed by Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. Iron Man first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39, which was published in March 1963. The character is a founding member of the Avengers. Iron Man is a billionaire inventor, philanthropist, and industrialist.
